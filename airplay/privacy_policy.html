Thank you for using AirPlay Receiver. We take your privacy and personal data protection seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles user information, including data collected by advertising services, and the rights available to you. Please read this policy carefully.

1. App Features

AirPlay Receiver is an AirPlay screen receiver that turns your Android device into an AirPlay endpoint. The main features include:

  • Receiving media content sent via AirPlay protocol from iPhone, iPad, Mac, and other Apple devices
  • Playing received audio, video, and photo content on your Android device
  • Supporting Screen Mirroring functionality
  • Providing media playback controls
  • Displaying ads to support the free use of the app

2. Data Collection

2.1 Data We Do Not Collect

AirPlay Receiver does not collect any personal user data.

Specifically:

  • No personal identity information: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information
  • No media content: Media content transmitted via AirPlay is played locally on your device only and is never uploaded or stored on any external server
  • No location data: Our app does not collect your precise geographic location
  • No usage data: We do not collect app usage statistics or analytics data

2.2 Data Collected by Advertising Services

Important: This app integrates Google AdMob advertising services. AdMob may collect certain information for ad delivery and analytics purposes.

Information AdMob may collect includes:

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • Network information: IP address, network type
  • Ad interaction data: Ad clicks and impression data
  • Approximate location: General geographic location based on IP address (used for localized ads)
  • App information: App package name and version number

How this data is used:

  • Displaying relevant personalized advertisements
  • Measuring ad effectiveness
  • Fraud prevention and abuse detection
  • Improving ad service quality

4. Network Permissions

AirPlay Receiver requires network permissions to enable the following functionality:

  • Appearing as an AirPlay receiver on the local network for Apple devices to discover
  • Broadcasting the service on the local network via mDNS/Bonjour protocol
  • Receiving media content transmitted from Apple devices on the local network
  • Connecting to AdMob ad servers to fetch and display advertisements

Network usage details:

  • Core screen casting features use only the local network and do not connect to the internet
  • Advertising functionality requires an internet connection to fetch ad content
  • Aside from advertising services, the app does not send data to any external servers

5. Data Storage and Processing

  • Temporary cache: To ensure smooth media playback, the app may temporarily cache some media data locally on your device. This cache is automatically cleared after playback ends
  • Playback history: The app does not save any playback history
  • Settings data: Basic app settings (such as volume and display preferences) are stored locally on your device only
  • Ad cache: AdMob may temporarily cache ad content on your device to improve loading speed
