How it works

Three steps to stream

1

Install & Open

Download AirPlay Receiver from Google Play and open the app. Both devices must be on the same Wi-Fi network.

2

Tap AirPlay on iPhone

On your iPhone, iPad, or Mac, open Control Center and tap the AirPlay icon. Select "AirPlay Receiver" from the list.

3

Enjoy

Your content streams instantly to your Android device. Control playback from either device at any time.
