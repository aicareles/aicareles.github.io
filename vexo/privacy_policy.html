English Version

Welcome to Vexo - Video Editor. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Vexo - Video Editor is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to external servers.

Important: All video processing operations are performed locally on your device. Your videos, photos, and audio files never leave your device.

2. App Features and Data Processing

Our app provides the following features, all processed locally:

  • Video Compression
  • Video Format Conversion
  • Video Merging
  • Audio and Video Merging
  • Audio Extraction from Video
  • Video Cropping
  • Video Speed Adjustment
  • Watermark Removal
  • Aspect Ratio Modification
  • Video to GIF Conversion

3. Permissions Required

To provide our services, the app requires the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To read and save video files on your device
  • Media Access: To access your photos and videos for editing

These permissions are only used for the core functionality of the app and data remains on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your privacy is fully protected.

5. Data Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device, your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keep your device's operating system updated
  • Use device encryption if available
  • Set a secure lock screen password

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages as it does not collect personal data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our servers. You have full control over your data on your device.
