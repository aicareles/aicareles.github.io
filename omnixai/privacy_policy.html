Privacy Policy

Omnix AI

Last Updated: December 8, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Omnix AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection when you use Omnix AI.

Information We Collect

Information We Do Not Collect

Omnix AI does not collect, store, or retain any personal user data.

We do not collect personal identification information, store account credentials, track your location, collect usage analytics, store conversation history, retain uploaded content, use tracking technologies, or share data with third-party advertisers.

Information Processed Temporarily

When you use Omnix AI's features, your data is processed temporarily to provide the requested service:

  • Text Summarization — Text content is sent to Google's Gemini AI for processing
  • Image Analysis — Images are transmitted to Gemini API for analysis
  • AI Image Generation — Your prompts are processed to generate images
  • Image Editing — Images and editing instructions are processed
  • Intelligent Chat — Your messages are sent to Gemini for responses
  • Document Processing (upcoming) — Documents will be processed for AI-powered features
Important

All data processing happens in real-time. Once your request is complete, we do not retain any of your content on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI via Firebase

Omnix AI uses Google's Gemini AI model through Firebase Generative AI to power all AI features. When you use our app, your content is transmitted to Google's servers, processed using Gemini AI, and the result is returned to you through our app. Neither we nor Google store your content after processing.

According to Google's policy, data sent to Gemini API is not used to train Google's models, is not retained after processing, and is processed according to Google's security standards. For more information, please review Google Privacy Policy and Firebase Terms of Service.

No Other Third Parties

We do not integrate any other third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

How We Use Your Data

Since we don't collect or store data, we cannot use it for any purpose beyond providing immediate AI services. Your data is processed only to fulfill your immediate request, transmitted securely to Gemini API, not stored on our servers, not used for analytics or marketing, not shared with anyone except Google's AI service, and not used to train our own models.

Data Security

While we don't store your data, we take security seriously. All data transmitted between your device and Google's servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. We don't store sensitive data on your device, leverage Firebase's enterprise-grade security infrastructure, and don't require account creation, eliminating password-related risks.

Your Rights and Choices

What You Control

You decide what content to input into the app. You can uninstall the app at any time. Since we don't create accounts or store data, there's nothing to delete.

Data Requests

Since we don't collect or store personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. We cannot provide data portability or fulfill data deletion requests.

Children's Privacy

Omnix AI does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app can be used by anyone. However, parental supervision is recommended for minors, and children should not input personal information into AI prompts.

International Data Transfers

When you use Omnix AI, your data is transmitted to Google's servers, which may be located worldwide. Google processes data according to their global infrastructure. Data transfer is necessary to provide AI services, and all transfers are protected by encryption.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features, changes in legal requirements, or updates to third-party services. When we make changes, the "Last Updated" date will be revised, significant changes will be communicated through the app, and continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases, payment information is handled entirely by Google Play. We never see or store your payment details. Google Play's privacy policy applies to billing information, and purchase records are maintained by Google, not us.

Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations. Since we don't collect data, most data protection obligations don't apply, but we maintain transparency about how your data flows through our app.

Limitations of Liability

While we strive to protect your privacy, we cannot control Google's data practices. We are not responsible for content you choose to input. You should not input sensitive personal information. AI-generated content may be inaccurate; use at your own discretion.

Your Responsibilities

When using Omnix AI, please don't input highly sensitive personal information, credentials, passwords, or financial data. Don't input content you want to keep completely private. Understand that data goes to Google's AI for processing, review Google's privacy policy, and use the app responsibly and ethically.

Future Features

As we add new AI capabilities, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly. Our core principle remains: we don't collect or store your data. All processing will continue through Google's Gemini AI, and you'll be notified of significant privacy-related changes.
