Introduction

Welcome to OmniDoc AI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection when you use OmniDoc AI.

Information We Collect

Information We Do Not Collect

OmniDoc AI does not collect, store, or retain any personal user data.

We do not collect personal identification information, store account credentials, track your location, collect usage analytics, store conversation history, retain uploaded content, use tracking technologies, or share data with third-party advertisers.

Information Processed Temporarily

When you use OmniDoc AI's features, your data is processed temporarily to provide the requested service:

  • PDF Merge & Split — PDF files are processed locally on your device to merge or split pages. No content is sent to external servers.
  • Image to PDF — Images are converted to PDF entirely on your device. No content leaves your phone.
  • PDF Watermark Removal — PDF pages are rendered as images and transmitted to Replicate's AI API for watermark removal, then returned to you immediately.
  • Image Watermark Removal — Images and mask data are transmitted to Replicate's LaMa AI API for inpainting, then returned to you immediately.
  • Image Enhancement & Upscaling — Images are transmitted to Replicate's Real-ESRGAN AI API for super-resolution processing, then returned to you immediately.
Important

All AI-powered processing happens in real-time via Replicate's API. Once your request is complete, neither we nor Replicate retain your content. Local features (PDF merge, split, image to PDF) are processed entirely on your device and never leave it.

Third-Party Services

Replicate AI API

OmniDoc AI uses Replicate's API to power AI features including watermark removal and image enhancement. When you use these features, your images or documents are transmitted to Replicate's servers, processed by AI models (LaMa, Real-ESRGAN), and the result is returned to you. Replicate does not retain your content after processing.

For more information, please review Replicate's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Billing

If you make in-app purchases, payment information is handled entirely by Google Play. We never see or store your payment details. Google Play's privacy policy applies to all billing information, and purchase records are maintained by Google, not us.

No Other Third Parties

We do not integrate any analytics tools, advertising networks, or other third-party tracking services.

How We Use Your Data

Since we don't collect or store data, we cannot use it for any purpose beyond providing immediate services. Your data is processed only to fulfill your immediate request, transmitted securely to Replicate's API where applicable, not stored on our servers, not used for analytics or marketing, and not used to train our own models.
